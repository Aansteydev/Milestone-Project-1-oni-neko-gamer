I have been writing as Oni Neko Gamer for around three years, I began writing at the beginning of the global pandemic as a way to take my mind away from what was happening around the world and to sharpen my writing skills whilst writing about something that I love doing, playing video games! I began writing on Wordpress since it as an easy to use development tool for new and veteran coders a like, after examining the code on my work it sparked my interest in learning how to do it for myself! I am currently undertaking a Level 5 Diploma in Fullstack Web Development and with my new skills I am going to migrate all of my previous work to a new, jazzier more responsive site. I mostly focus on retro titles and import games mostly due to growing up in the U.K and watching enviously at games that did not see a release here! I also enjoy writing about Anime, Arcade sticks and modifying them as well as a host of other topics such as what I am playing currently and retrospectives on whole game series. Gaming is a passion for me and something that I don't think that I will ever grow tired off, it is an art form that is just as valid as Cinema and Animation so I hope that you follow me on my journey to spill my thoughts on the topic out into the internet.
