I have been writing as Oni Neko Gamer for around three years, it started
during the global pandemic as a way to take my mind off of things and
to write about something that I have been passionate
about since I was a child, Video Games! I have been playing
Video Games for as long as I can remember,
which is a normal story for people of my vintage!

I write about import retro games more than western titles,
what interests me the most about them is that they weren't released locally
so it feels a bit like you're playing a game that no one else has seen!
My journey into import titles has lead me to begin to learn Japanese
I am nowhere near the level needed to play them without a guide
but I get better at it every time I try! much like coding
